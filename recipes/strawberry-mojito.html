Strawberry Mojito

This mojito mocktail recipe is sweet and refreshing making it an excellent choice for summer.This strawberry mojito is just as fun to make as it is to drink. It is easy, healthy and low in sugar making it perfect for all of you gym folks.

Ingredients

  • 3 strawberries sliced
  • 10 mint leaves
  • 1 cup soda water
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (or sweetener of choice)
  • extra mint and strawberries to serve

Steps

  1. Muddle strawberries and sugar(or monkfruit sweetner) in a glass to release the juices. Try and get the strawberry pieces small enough so that they will fit through a straw. Use a blender in this step if preferred.
  2. Add the lime juice and stir to combine.
  3. Clap the mint together in your hands to release the scent (you'll know it's worked when your hands smell minty!)
  4. Fill the glass with ice and add soda water. Stir to combine
  5. Garnish with extra mint and strawberry slices. Then Enjoy!
