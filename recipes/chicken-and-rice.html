Yogurt Bowl

Description

This sweet, delicious chicken and rice embodies the soul of a gym bro or sis. And the best thing about it is that it only has a handful of ingredients so you can be sure to not spend time faffing around with your cupboards trying to find every ingredient known to man.

If you follow this simple recipe you can be sure to give your biceps a kiss as they will love you for providing them with the high protein they will receive.

Ingredients

  • chicken thigh
  • soy souce
  • honey
  • cornflour
  • white rice
  • broccoli
  • chilli powder
  • salt
    •

    Steps

    1. Use a rice cooker to cook your rice
    2. cut your chicken into small pieces
    3. marinate with soy sauce, honey, chilli powder and cornflour
    4. cook chicken in airfryer for 12 minutes at 180 degrees celcius
    5. halfway through cooking process, brush on more marination to enhance the flavour
    6. Cut up broccoli and stick in steamer for around 20 minutes
    7. When everything is cooked, stick it on a plate
    8. Last but not least ENJOY
