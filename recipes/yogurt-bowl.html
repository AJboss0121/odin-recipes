Yogurt Bowl

About the yogurt Bowl

The yogurt bowl should be the staple in any persons diet. Filled with healthy goodness top-to-bottom, and as easy as throwing a few ingredients together, Why would you refuse to create this beauty? This recipe consists of a rich layer of yogurt mixed with a protein powder of your choice (vanilla is my favorite). Then a heap of rolled outs for texture is layered on top along with some of your favorite fruit and chocolate.

A limitless amount of variables exist for this meal, whether you choose to add or remove toppings is completely up to you.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt (preferably a high protein one)
  • rolled oats
  • Fruit (of your choice)
  • More fruit
  • MORE FRUIT
  • Dark chocolate

Steps

  1. Add around 500g of yogurt to a bowl
  2. Add approximately 100g of rolled oats, layered on top of the yogurt
  3. Add fruit, frozen fruit adds a texture thar resembles ice cream but fresh fruit will taste better
  4. Top it all off with some chunks of dark chocolate for a rich deep flavour
